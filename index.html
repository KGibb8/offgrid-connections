Preflight checklist

  • 2 phones or other hotspots, with no internet connection
  • SSID and passwords of the hotspot WiFi
  • USB Sticks with Linux, Mac and Windows installers
  • Turn the internet off!
  • Projector working? I hope so!

Already Scuttling?

Any existing users, we're going to start new IDs. So please to a backup of your .ssb folder, or sit out!

  • Open a terminal...
  • $ mv .ssb .ssb.backup

(When we've finished and you want to go back...)

  • $ rm -rf .ssb
  • $ mv .ssb.backup .ssb
